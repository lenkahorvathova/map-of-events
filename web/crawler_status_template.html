What does Cause of Failure code stands for?
  • event_html_not_downloaded - failed to download event's HTML content (probably faulty event's URL)
  • event_data_not_parsed - failed to parse data from event's HTML (probably missing title or date)
  • event_datetime_not_processed - failed to process event's date (probably doesn't match format for its parser)
  • event_gps_not_acquired - failed to acquire event's GPS (probably missing or ambiguous location)
